What is soft elegance?

A celebration of the sweet, soft, sensitive, and care free lady. Soft elegance is can be defined as the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner. The ability to tastefully combine fine or luxurious pieces, style, design, and elegant furnishings. Elegance in itself means being gracefully refined and dignified, as in tastes, habits, or literary style: examples being an elegant young lady; an elegant prosodist. an aesthetic.
