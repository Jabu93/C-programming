What is soft elegance?

A celebration of being sweet, soft, sensitive, and care free. Soft elegance is can be defined as the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner. The ability to tastefully combine fine or luxurious pieces, style, design, and elegant furnishings. Elegance in itself can mean being gracefully refined and dignified, as in tastes, habits, or literary style: examples being an elegant young lady; an elegant prosodist, an aesthetic.
